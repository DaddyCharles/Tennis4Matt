============================================================

   SETTING UP SMS IN IVAN

============================================================

This lets you text your students directly from Ivan.

Cost: about $0.08 per text message.


------------------------------------------------------------
   STEP 1  -  Create a Twilio account (free)
------------------------------------------------------------

   - Go to:  twilio.com
   - Click "Sign Up" - it is free to start
   - You get $15 credit to try it out


------------------------------------------------------------
   STEP 2  -  Get an Australian phone number
------------------------------------------------------------

   - In Twilio Console, click "Phone Numbers"
   - Click "Buy a Number"
   - Search for Australia (+61)
   - Buy one - costs about $2/month


------------------------------------------------------------
   STEP 3  -  Get your credentials
------------------------------------------------------------

   In Twilio Console, on the home page you will see:

      Account SID  (starts with AC...)
      Auth Token   (click to reveal)

   Copy both of these.


------------------------------------------------------------
   STEP 4  -  Enter in Ivan
------------------------------------------------------------

   - Open Ivan in your browser
   - Go to Settings (bottom of left menu)
   - Scroll to "SMS / Twilio" section
   - Paste your Account SID
   - Paste your Auth Token
   - Enter your Twilio phone number (e.g. +61480123456)
   - Click "Test Connection"

   You should receive a test text on your phone!


------------------------------------------------------------
   STEP 5  -  You're ready
------------------------------------------------------------

   - Go to SMS in the left menu
   - Add your students as contacts
   - Create groups (e.g. "Monday Group")
   - Start sending!
