============================================================

   USING IVAN ON YOUR PHONE

============================================================

Ivan runs on your computer, but you can open it on your
phone too - as long as your phone is on the SAME Wi-Fi as
your computer.

This is great for checking your schedule or marking lessons
complete while you're out on the court.


------------------------------------------------------------
   STEP 1  -  Make sure Ivan is running
------------------------------------------------------------

   On your computer, start Ivan (double-click 2_START.bat).
   Leave it running.


------------------------------------------------------------
   STEP 2  -  Find your computer's IP address
------------------------------------------------------------

   WINDOWS:
   - Press the Windows key, type "cmd", press Enter
   - In the black window type:   ipconfig
   - Look for "IPv4 Address" - it looks like:
        192.168.1.42

   MAC:
   - Open System Settings > Wi-Fi > Details
   - Look for "IP Address" - it looks like:
        192.168.1.42


------------------------------------------------------------
   STEP 3  -  Open Ivan on your phone
------------------------------------------------------------

   - Make sure your phone is on the same Wi-Fi as the computer
   - Open your phone's web browser (Safari or Chrome)
   - In the address bar type your IP address, then :9999

        Example:   192.168.1.42:9999

   Ivan opens on your phone!


------------------------------------------------------------
   TIP  -  Add Ivan to your home screen
------------------------------------------------------------

   IPHONE (Safari):
   - Tap the Share button (square with an arrow)
   - Tap "Add to Home Screen"

   ANDROID (Chrome):
   - Tap the three dots (top right)
   - Tap "Add to Home screen"

   Now Ivan has its own icon, just like a real app.


------------------------------------------------------------
   PHONE NOTIFICATIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

   Ivan can send alerts to your phone (e.g. "lights on soon").
   In Ivan, go to Settings > Notifications and turn on
   "Push Notifications" while viewing Ivan on your phone.
